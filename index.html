Our Story

Surina Masala Kya Hai?

Surina Masala sirf ek brand nahi, ek bharosa hai. Jamtara (Jharkhand) se shuru hui yeh journey ek hi lakshya ke saath aage badh rahi hai: Har ghar mein asli, bina milawat wala masala pahunchana.

Market mein milne wale masalo mein synthetic colors aur sawdust ki milawat aam baat hai. Humne decide kiya ki hum apna production khud karenge. Cold-grinding technology use karke, hum masalo ke natural oils aur aroma ko protect karte hain.
